Kodak BW400 · 35mm · cyanotype on watercolour paper

Someone went somewhere
by the water.

the first row
Someone standing in front of a pier, faded like a memory. A tram driving by on the road. A suburban street. Who lives here? Hidden by shrubery, a small white building stands strong.
the middle row
A busy market street, what are the people looking to buy? People boarding their sailboats. It's a nice day for a swim. The mast of a larger vessel behind them. Open water stretching back. A woman standing alone at the pier. Who is she smiling at behind the camera?
the last row
Brick buildings with bright white shutters, what country might this be in? A bustling city street. Everyone has someplace important to be. Two friends sharing a loaf of bread. They must be hungry. A sunny day at the carnival swings, children finally learning how to fly.
